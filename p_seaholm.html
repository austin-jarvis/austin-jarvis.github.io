Seaholm Transformer Site 2015 Austin, TX NADAAA

A vanity fence in Austin, TX masks an electrical step-down site adjacent to the new city library. The fence is composed of incrementally rotated concrete posts of five different colors. Undulating along the length of the sidewalk, the fence compresses and expands the promenades to create difference along the perimeter of what is usually a banal piece of urban infrastructure.
