Arts Management 2016-2018 Houston, TX Client: Polk Street Studios

With Polk Street Studios, the public art office of artist Patrick Renner, I've consulted on many architectural coordination issues. For the studio's clients I've produced varied presentation materials critical to securing work and illustrating the yet unrealized final product. For the artists I've helped digest and coordinate the architectural context in the form of schematic drawings and physical templates critical to the succesful design and installation of sculptures.
