Creative Infrastructure 2017-2022 Houston, TX Client: Metalab Studio

Various works with Metalab Studio and RE:site Art. The public projects of these design studios straddle the line of architecture, artwork, and infrastructure. The projects illustrated here serve to grant identity to public programming. In Green Bay, WI, a sculpture of a fallen tree forms the entry portal into a botanic garden. In Omaha, NE, a parabolic array of suspension cables support a network of pedestrain walkways. In Richmond, TX, a massive Fermat's Spiral filters the sun for a firestation.

Personal involvement: various stages of SD, DD, and CD, project management, parametric optimization, & presentation materials.
